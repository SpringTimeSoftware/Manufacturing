# Help Glossary

Run: HELP-SYSTEM-AND-ACTION-COMPLETION-01

| Term | Definition | Related Screens |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Draft | A record that is being prepared and is not yet approved for operational use. | Items; BOM Library; Routings; Price Lists |
| Released | A record version that can be used by downstream planning or execution according to lifecycle rules. | BOM Library; Routings; Work Orders |
| On hold | A controlled state that blocks or limits use until an authorized review clears the reason. | Customers; Suppliers; Quality; Inventory |
| Approval | A role-controlled decision that records who approved or rejected a business change. | Approvals; Workflow & Numbering; Audit Trail |
| Audit | A recorded business event showing actor, time, entity, action, and outcome. | Audit Trail; Users; Roles & Permissions |
| Traceability | The ability to follow an item, lot, serial, source, and shipment through production, quality, inventory, and dispatch. | Traceability; Quality; Dispatch; Production Receipt |
| Price list | A governed set of item prices by currency, UOM, quantity break, and effective period. | Price Lists; Customers; Sales Orders |
| Discount scheme | A governed set of discount rules that can apply by customer, item, quantity, or commercial segment. | Discount Schemes; Price Lists; Sales Orders |
| Tax category | A controlled commercial value used to determine tax behavior on sales or purchase records. | Tax, Currency & Terms; Customers; Suppliers; Items |
| Payment terms | A controlled value that defines expected payment timing and discount behavior. | Tax, Currency & Terms; Customers; Suppliers |

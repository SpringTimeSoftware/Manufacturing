# Engineering Screen Specification

## Engineering Rework Principles

Engineering screens preserve the manufacturing backbone, but they need deeper editor behavior, revision control, attachments, approval governance, and downstream visibility. Do not replace the current BOM/routing/ECO backbone destructively. Extend it with V2 controls and compatibility adapters where needed.

## Downstream Dependency Rules

- Released BOM/routing changes must show impact on open SOs, MPS, MRP, WOs, job cards, purchase demand, QC plans, and dispatch risk where applicable.
- Component UOM and item tracking rules must come from the V2 item master, not ad hoc fields.
- Engineering documents must be visible on web and mobile execution screens after release.
